About Us

Main Line Math Project is a free community-based organization that is dedicated to providing students with a safe, helpful, and enjoyable environment to learn and grow. As high schoolers, our tutors understand the anxiety and stress that students face during tutoring sessions. Main Line Math Project has made it our mission to mitigate these feelings while simultaneously focusing on the improvement of students' conceptual and procedural understanding of specific concepts. Our tutors have all exceeded mathematically and have an in-depth understanding of concepts related to your child's coursework. Having taken the courses your child is enrolled in, we know the way educators teach specific topics and understand how to build/reinforce these problem-solving methods. We are dedicated to your child's success and are excited to assist them in their academic journey!

Although we run our program in the Ludington Library, we are not affiliated with it.
